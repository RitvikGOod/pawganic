🚀 Grow Pawganic — Tips for You

Your roadmap to scaling the business to every dog home!

01

📱 Social Media is Your BFF

Post daily on Instagram & Facebook — dogs + cute content = viral reach. Use reels showing your baking process and dogs enjoying the treats. Live baking sessions build massive trust!

02

🤝 Partner with Vets & Pet Stores

Leave sample packs with local vets, pet groomers, and pet supply stores. A vet recommendation is worth more than any ad. Offer them a referral commission.

03

🎁 Subscription Boxes

Launch a monthly treat subscription — "Pawganic Treat Box". Customers subscribe and receive fresh treats monthly. Predictable income + loyal customers = business stability.

04

🛒 Sell on Marketplace Platforms

List on Instamojo, Amazon, Flipkart, and pet-focused platforms like Heads Up For Tails. This expands your reach beyond your city with zero extra marketing cost.

05

🐾 Dog Events & Pop-Ups

Attend dog shows, pet fairs and community events. Set up a cute stall, offer free samples, and collect contact details. Word-of-mouth from these events is gold.

06

💌 Email & WhatsApp Marketing

Build a list of customers and send monthly updates, new flavour launches, seasonal offers, and loyalty discounts. A WhatsApp broadcast channel can replace expensive SMS marketing.

07

🏷️ Get FSSAI Certified

Getting food-safety certification builds enormous trust, lets you sell in physical retail stores, and opens doors to bulk B2B orders from pet chains and corporate gifting.

08

🌟 Influencer Collaborations

Find popular dog Instagram accounts and send free treat samples. Their audience trusts their recommendations and even 1 post can drive hundreds of orders.

09

🎨 Brand Packaging

Invest in beautiful, branded packaging with your Pawganic logo. Good packaging makes people photograph their orders and share on social media — free marketing every order!
