FAQs 🦴

Common questions from curious dog parents!

Are your treats safe for all dog breeds?
Yes! All our treats are made with dog-safe ingredients suitable for all breeds. However, if your dog has specific allergies, please mention it in your order and we'll customise accordingly.
How long do the treats stay fresh?
Our treats stay fresh for 2–3 weeks when stored in a cool, dry place. For maximum freshness, store in an airtight container. We never use preservatives, so freshness depends on proper storage.
Do you ship across India?
Yes! We ship pan-India. Orders typically arrive within 3–5 business days. Hyderabad orders get same/next-day delivery.
Can I order custom treats or shapes?
Absolutely! We love custom orders — paw shapes, bone shapes, your dog's name, specific flavours — just mention it in your message and we'll make it happen.
Do you offer bulk or corporate gifting orders?
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders (10+ packs) and corporate gifting. Perfect for pet-friendly offices or event giveaways. Contact us for pricing.
